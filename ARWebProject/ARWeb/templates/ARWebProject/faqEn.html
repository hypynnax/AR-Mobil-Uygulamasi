Frequently Asked Questions
What is the purpose of this application? +
This application allows children to learn about different topics in a fun and interactive way. Through Augmented Reality, they can view various digital elements in the real world.
What is Augmented Reality (AR)? +
AR is a technology that adds digital elements to the real world. With this app, children can see 3D objects and animations in their actual environment by using the tablet or phone camera.
What age group is this application suitable for? +
This app is generally designed for children aged 6-12. Parental supervision is recommended for younger children.
Is an internet connection required to use the app? +
Yes, some content requires Wi-Fi or mobile data to work. However, certain features are available offline.
Is this app safe for children? +
Yes, the app is designed to provide safe content for children and does not contain any harmful material.
How do children use the app? +
After downloading the app, children can use the tablet or phone camera to see various 3D objects and animations by pointing to specific locations and can also play interactive games.
Is the app free? +
The basic features of the app are free, but there is a paid version to access more content and some special features.
What type of content does the app have? +
The app offers AR-supported content on various topics like animals, nature, science, and mythology, helping children learn while having fun.
Does the app include any games? +
Yes, there are games related to certain topics. For example, children can feed virtual animals or complete tasks in science-themed mini-games.
How does the app contribute to children's learning? +
The app reinforces what children learn in school through augmented reality and helps them discover new topics.
Will my child be exposed to screens for too long? +
The app is designed to offer short activities. Parents can monitor screen time and encourage breaks when needed.
Does my child need to stay in one place while using the app? +
Yes, for safety, it is important for children to stay in one place while using the app. They are encouraged to keep the device steady and avoid running or moving.
Do I need a specific device to use all app features? +
Most modern smartphones and tablets support augmented reality. However, some features may not work properly on older devices.
Is it safe for my child to use the app alone? +
Yes, although the content is appropriate for children, parental guidance is recommended, especially for younger children.
Does this app help with topics my child learns at school? +
Yes, some content supports school curriculum topics, allowing children to see their knowledge in action.
Can my child contribute content to the app? +
Currently, user-generated content is not available, but new features may be added with future updates.
Does the app take up a lot of storage? +
The app size may vary with additional content. To avoid large storage use, unnecessary files can be periodically cleared.
In what language are AR contents provided? +
The app's content is currently available in Turkish and English. Other languages may be added in the future.
Does this app help children develop social skills? +
Yes, the app encourages children's curiosity and allows them to socialize by sharing experiences with family or working in groups for some activities.
Are there plans to release new content in the future? +
Yes, new content is regularly added by the development team, allowing children to continuously learn new information.
